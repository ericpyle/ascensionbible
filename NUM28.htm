1 Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying, 2 “Command the children of Israel, and tell them, ‘See that you present my offering, my food for my offerings made by fire, of a pleasant aroma to me, in their due season.’ 3 You shall tell them, ‘This is the offering made by fire which you shall offer to Yahweh: male lambs a year old without defect, two day by day, for a continual ascension*Hebrew עֹלָה (`olah). Typically rendered “burnt offering(s)” in English Bibles. . 4 You shall offer the one lamb in the morning, and you shall offer the other lamb at evening; 5 with one tenth of an ephah†1 ephah is about 22 liters or about 2/3 of a bushel of fine flour for a meal offering, mixed with the fourth part of a hin of beaten oil. 6 It is a continual ascension‡Hebrew עֹלָה (`olah). Typically rendered “burnt offering(s)” in English Bibles. , which was ordained in Mount Sinai for a pleasant aroma, an offering made by fire to Yahweh. 7 Its drink offering shall be the fourth part of a hin for the one lamb. You shall pour out a drink offering of strong drink to Yahweh in the holy place. 8 The other lamb you shall offer at evening. As the meal offering of the morning, and as its drink offering, you shall offer it, an offering made by fire, of a pleasant aroma to Yahweh.

Typically rendered “burnt offering(s)” in English Bibles. of a pleasant aroma, an offering made by fire to Yahweh. 14 Their drink offerings shall be half a hin of wine for a bull, and the third part of a hin for the ram, and the fourth part of a hin for a lamb. This is the ascension†Hebrew עֹלָה (`olah). Typically rendered “burnt offering(s)” in English Bibles. of every month throughout the months of the year. 15 One male goat for a sin offering to Yahweh; it shall be offered besides the continual ascension‡Hebrew עֹלָה (`olah). 16 “ ‘In the first month, on the fourteenth day of the month, is Yahweh’s Passover. 17 On the fifteenth day of this month shall be a feast. Unleavened bread shall be eaten for seven days. 18 In the first day shall be a holy convocation. You shall do no regular work; 19 but you shall offer an offering made by fire, an ascension§Hebrew עֹלָה (`olah). Typically rendered “burnt offering(s)” in English Bibles. to Yahweh: two young bulls, and one ram, and seven male lambs a year old; they shall be to you without defect; 20 and their meal offering, fine flour mixed with oil. You shall offer three tenths for a bull, and two tenths for the ram. 21 You shall offer one tenth for every lamb of the seven lambs; 22 and one male goat for a sin offering, to make atonement for you. 23 You shall offer these besides the ascension*Hebrew עֹלָה (`olah). Typically rendered “burnt offering(s)” in English Bibles. of the morning, which is for a continual ascension†Hebrew עֹלָה (`olah). Typically rendered “burnt offering(s)” in English Bibles. , and its drink offering. 25 On the seventh day you shall have a holy convocation. You shall do no regular work.
26 “ ‘Also in the day of the first fruits, when you offer a new meal offering to Yahweh in your feast of weeks, you shall have a holy convocation. You shall do no regular work; 27 but you shall offer an ascension§Hebrew עֹלָה (`olah).
